"Who the hell are you?"


Nivritti Associates LLP was founded to provide need-based business consulting , training processes, training workshops and seminars to help individuals and leaders at all levels of an organisation.

Nivritti is derived from the Sanskrit root words, Ni, meaning “in” or “inward,” and vritti, representing citta vritti, or the mind's thoughts. Nivritti is the path of turning inward and is, therefore, the path of development, prioritizing, meditation and contemplation. On this path, attention is directed inward, often with a focus on acceptance and finding connection with the self. Highly transformational leaders focus their attention inwards for best results. Nivritti also refers to freedom, freedom to think beyond boundaries. Nivritti is comprised of experienced individuals each with 30+ years of industry experience. These senior partners are seasoned professionals with firsthand knowledge of human development principles and strategies learned in the trenches in their own careers.



"What does that even mean? What do you DO?"


Nivrriti is a boutique business consulting firm specializing in providing customized learning solutions with measurable outcomes, in a journey based format, and one off programs to address specific business and/or leadership challenges.


The senior partners, facilitators and coaches at Nivritti Associates LLP help you to think beyond, to achieve your outcomes, meet your KPA’s, address business/leadership/people challenges. The founders and senior partners of Nivritti have in the past conducted workshops and seminars across India in the critical areas of communication, leadership and self-development. At the core, these have been efforts to share their experiences, to make a difference to the lives of individuals with a drive to succeed. The programs have consistently received good feedback from the participants. Their methodology created and refined over the years, guarantees results. It is designed specifically for clients who have risen to the top of their disciplines in their professional and/or technical careers, and are now challenged to develop their full potential as revenue-generating, Business Development Professionals.
